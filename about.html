About Us

TULIP for Printing & Industry was founded in 1980, it is now considered one of the leading printing houses in Egypt specialized in Offset & Flexo Printing With three branches across Egypt, we serve multiple sectors including Label Printing such as: In Mould Label, Shrink Sleeve, Self Adhesive and Wet Strength & Wet Glue Labels. We also serve: Hotelier Media, Publications Houses, Restaurants and Medical Organisations

With top-notch technologically advanced pre-press systems in the market, we offer tailor-made solutions that have been developed over years of experience, in addition to the services offered by our technological partners,to ensure customer satisfaction.

We offer a comprehensive range of document production services in both offset, digital and flexo printing environments.

With the most updated technology in finishing equipment, we have adequate capacities in cutting, folding, collating and sewing. Regardless how large or small your project is, it will gain the utmost level of care and attention by our skillful finishing operators, ensuring the required result.
