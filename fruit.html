Solution

Technical Consulting

An on site team works tirelessly in the search for continuous product improvement, through improved file preparation. Our technicians provide the necessary web offset printing press expertise and act as a bridge between the customer and our company.


Color Management
We have designed a colour management system that allows RGB file management. This results in significantly improved ink coverage, greater fidelity of the printed results with greater commitment to the values of the original images as well as reduced times spent on customer file preparation as CMYK conversion is unnecessary. In addition, we are using eXact; the fastest path to make-ready, from the ink kitchen to the pressroom. It is considered as a next generation hand-held color measurement solution that takes into account the new business needs and dynamics of a global supply chain
